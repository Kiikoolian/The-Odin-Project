Onion Stew

Description

Ingredients

  • 6 onions, chopped
  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cups cold water
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • Salt
  • Cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup grated edam cheese

Steps

  1. Saute the Onions: In a large soup kettle, saut√© the chopped onions in the butter for 5 minutes or until they're translucent.
  2. Add Flour and Stir: Remove the kettle from the burner and sprinkle the flour over the onions. Stir in with a wire whisk to combine.
  3. Simmer and Blend: Slowly add the water, stirring with the whisk continuously to avoid lumps. Return the kettle to the burner and simmer on medium low for about 30 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Blend Small Quantities: Blend small quantities at a time in a food processor or blender and return to the soup kettle.
  5. Add Milk and Egg Yolk: Slowly add the milk, stirring with the whisk. Beat the egg yolk briefly with the whisk.
  6. Add Edam Cheese and Salt: Slowly add about 1/2 cup of the soup to the egg and blend well. Add salt and cayenne pepper to taste.
  7. Serve and Enjoy: Grate edam cheese over the soup and serve.
